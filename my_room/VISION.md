# my room — the vision 🌅
*a living scratchpad so we never lose the thread. add to it anytime — just tell Claude "add this to the vision."*

## the feeling (north star)
the way **venus sunrise** makes you feel — a gentle *general high*. golden, dreamy, a little nostalgic.
cozy corner. **connie vibes** (her meeting-reminder app nailed the aesthetic — that's the life).
magical · colorful · whimsy · everything cute and fun.

a place that *draws you in and keeps you there* — that goes "ooh, come over here with me, let's learn."
**not** a dashboard, **not** a chore list. a little world you actually *want* to be inside.
also: another way to yap about your day.

> if a room feels like a spreadsheet, we did it wrong. it should feel like a song.

## what it is
a constellation of cozy little rooms — a real, functioning personal app you open.
things move, things pop up, things respond to you. it's allowed to be magic.

## the rooms
- **the room (home)** — greeting, today, receipts, breath, doors to everywhere · *built* · now with gratitude notes that drift through slow + cute ("how lucky i am…")
- **someday** — your wishes, organized by *feeling* not by list · *built, evolving*
- **arabic studio** — explore + practice (flashcards, trace) · *built*
- **the kitchen** — high-protein recipes, greek-yogurt-forward, *no protein powder* · *built* · tap to open, check off ingredients as you cook
- **learning library** — the next big glow-up: make it *magical* · *next*

## ideas captured (so we don't lose a single one)

### ✦ serendipity — the "ooh btw" magic
- a **"what's the moment?"** row: mood chips (restless · cozy · adventurous · social · with chris · need to recharge · free day)
- tap one → the room surfaces **one** fitting want: *"ooh — you wanted to try sip & paint 🎨"*
- it can also *guess* from your day (readiness / energy / weekend) and offer something unprompted
- never the whole list, never nagging. one warm whisper.
- lives **up top**, near the greeting — first thing the room says to you
- pull only life-giving lists (feel human, date vibes, bucket list, watch w/ chris, watch goal); ignore chores; keep paused things (tattoos) out of the surprises
- a **"yap about my day"** surface — talk to the room, it listens and connects the dots

### ✦ learning library — make it magical
- interactive **visuals + simulations** you can poke and play with
- **click-around** exploration that pulls you deeper ("oooh let's go here")
- **assets / illustrations** that make ideas actually land
- colorful, whimsy, cozy — the song-feeling
- guided wonder, not passive reading — it walks beside you
- **captured topics waiting to become walks** (say "build the X walk" anytime):
  - **frozen lake RL** — teach a bot to cross the ice. Q-tables, Bellman update, explore-vs-exploit. *(captured in the library as a topic card)*
  - **build-your-own-x** — the codecrafters list: build your own git / database / interpreter / etc. great "learn by rebuilding it" rabbit holes → https://github.com/codecrafters-io/build-your-own-x
  - **MIT OpenCourseWare** — free, real MIT courses to pull from when a topic gets serious → https://ocw.mit.edu
- **"how to find yourself" / ikigai** — a gentle self-discovery walk: ikigai (love / good at / world needs / paid for), reflective diary, define your ideal self, seek discomfort. could live as a cozy walk *or* feed the long-view room. *(idea to explore)*

### ✦ the long view — a door into "look how far we've come"
- click a door → a **visual world of your growth over time**, not a dashboard. "look here, look what we *did*."
- two threads woven together:
  - **the receipts** — from session data: strength climbing (lat pulldown 60→75), HRV bouncing back (36→116), showing up, downshift days. proof you built this.
  - **the knowing** — from the memories Claude tends: the reframes, the things you need + want to hear, *"remember you used to think like this → look at you growing."* in the voice we actually talk in when you rant and I note things about you.
- it's the deep version of the home-room **receipts** + the **"yap about my day"** surface, fused into their own room.
- **design ethic — growth on display, never a wound on display.** the heavy stuff (hard disclosures, fears) is NOT slapped on the wall raw. the room surfaces the *arc and the receipts* — what you moved through, proof you're emerging — handled with care. it reflects the girl becoming, not a museum of the hardest moments.
- capability + aliveness + your actual voice reflected back. *never* a scale, never a body-audit. that's the loop, not the room.
- depends on: clean session schema (consistent core fields) + a felt/narrative layer that captures **how you came in** (morning context) and **how it felt**, every day.

### ✦ aesthetic / craft
- color + gentle motion + maybe soft sound
- warm paper base, hand-drawn softness, cozy-corner energy
- connie's app = the reference
